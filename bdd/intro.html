The Biodiversity Data Digitizer

The Biodiversity Data Digitizer (BDD) is an information system designed for the easy digitization, manipulation, and dissemination of quality biodiversity data. It stands out for allowing the user to manipulate its data simply and objectively, especially the data from field observations and small collections.

Data type supported:

Species occurrences
Species
Specimens interactions
Pollination deficit
Pollinator monitoring
Multimedia resources
Reference resources
